    About Us

    We Are A Set Of Well Trained And Professional Coaches, Whom Have Worked For Some Of The Best Clubs Around The World, We Have Started This Academy With The Aim Of Helping Young Aspiring Footballers Discover And Nurture Their Talents, We Also Have Contact Of Club Scouts Whom We Recommend Players To. Our Sole Aim Is To Bring The Best Out Of You.

    Henry's Academy

    It Was Founded By Mr Henry Chukwumaeze, On The 24th Of May, 2010. He Was A Lover Of Football And Had Deep Knowledge About The Game. He Started Coaching At Age 35, As A Junior Team Coach. He later Resigned And Decided To Start This Academy To Help Young Footballers Discover And Build On Their Talents.

    At This Prestigious Academy, We Offer Alot Starting From The:

    • Beginner To Master Fitness Training.
    • Modern Training Drills and Activities.
    • Exclusive Access To Team Accessory For Training And Special Trainers Meetings

    These And Many More Are Available At Our Academy, Please Bring your Children To Our Academy, So They Can Receive The Best Training From The Best Set Of Trainers.
